  <h1>Delicious Dumplings</h1>
  <p>Learn how to make tasty homemade dumplings with simple ingredients and easy steps!</p>

  <h2>Ingredients</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>2 cups all-purpose flour</li>
    <li>1/2 cup water</li>
    <li>1 cup minced vegetables or meat filling</li>
    <li>Salt and spices to taste</li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Instructions</h2>
  <ol>
    <li>Mix the flour and water to form dough.</li>
    <li>Roll the dough into small circles.</li>
    <li>Add the filling to each dough circle.</li>
    <li>Seal the edges and steam the dumplings.</li>
  </ol>
